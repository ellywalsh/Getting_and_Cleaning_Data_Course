Codebook -- Average Accelerometer Data Set

1. SUBJECT </br>
  The subject (#1-30) from whose phone data was collected
  
2. ACTIVITY </br>
  One of six activities for which data was recorded, including: </br>
    1 WALKING </br>
    2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS </br>
    3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS </br>
    4 SITTING </br>
    5 STANDING </br>
    6 LAYING </br>
